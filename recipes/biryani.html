Detailed Chicken Biryani Recipe

About this Recipe

Aromatic long-grain basmati rice layered over tender, marinated chicken, infused with whole spices and saffron, garnished with caramelized onions and fresh herbs. This traditional stovetop recipe delivers authentic flavors reminiscent of a classic Indian-style biryani.

Ingredients

  • Marinade:
    • 1 lb boneless skinless chicken thighs, halved
    • 2 tsp biryani masala
    • 1 tbsp ginger paste
    • 1 tbsp garlic paste
    • 2 tsp kosher salt
    • 1 tbsp Kashmiri red chili powder
    • ½ tsp ground turmeric
    • ¼ cup fresh mint leaves, chopped
    • 2 tbsp lemon juice
    • ¾ cup plain yogurt
  • Crispy Friend Onions:
    • 4 tbsp ghee
    • 1 large yellow onion, thinly sliced
  • Rice:
    • 2½ cups extra-long grain basmati rice
    • 1 tsp black cumin seeds (shahi jeera)
    • 2 bay leaves
    • 4 green cardamom pods
    • 8 cloves
