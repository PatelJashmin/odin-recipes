Detailed Dal Chawal Recipe

About this Recipe

A nourishing one-pot meal featuring perfectly cooked rice and creamy spiced lentils-this Dal Chawal is wholesome, cozy, and complete when paired with simple add-ons like papad and lemon.

Ingredients

For the Rice:

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp coconut oil
  • 3½ cups water

For the Dal (Lentils):

  • 1/2 cup toor dal
  • 1/2 cup masoor dal
  • 1 cup rice
  • 1 medium onion, finely chopped
  • 1 inch ginger, grated
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 green chili, chopped
  • 1 tsp cumin seeds
  • 1 pinch hing (asafoetida)
  • 1/2 cup tomato puree
  • 1/2 tsp turmeric powder
  • 1 tsp coriander powder
  • 1/2 tsp cumin powder
  • 1 tsp Kashmiri chili powder
  • 1/2 tsp amchur (dry mango powder)
  • 2 tbsp oil
  • Salt to taste
  • 4 cups water
  • Fresh cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. Cook the Rice:
    Rinse the basmati, then simmer with salt, coconut oil, and water for 15 minutes. Let it rest — this yields fluffy, separated grains.
  2. Prepare the Dal:
    Sauté oil, cumin seeds, onion, ginger, garlic, chili, and hing until fragrant. Add tomato puree and all spices, cooking until the mixture thickens. Then add soaked lentils with water and cook in a pressure cooker (15 mins electric, ~4 whistles on stovetop).
  3. Finish and Serve:
    Once pressure releases, stir in hot water to reach your preferred consistency, and mix in cilantro. Serve dal alongside rice and your choice of add-ons.
