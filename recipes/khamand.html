Detailed Khamand Recipe

About this Recipe

Khamand is a soft and spongy Gujarati snack made with gram flour and steamed to perfection. It's light, fluffy, and topped with a flavorful tempering of mustard seeds, curry leaves, and green chilies.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup gram flour (besan)
  • 1 tbsp semolina (sooji)
  • 1 tsp lemon juice
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp ginger-green chili paste
  • 1 tsp Eno (fruit salt)
  • Salt to taste
  • Water as needed
  • For tempering: 1 tbsp oil, ½ tsp mustard seeds, 1-2 green chilies, few curry leaves, 1 tsp sugar + ¼ cup water

Steps

  1. Mix gram flour, semolina, ginger-chili paste, lemon juice, sugar, salt, and water into a smooth batter.
  2. Add Eno just before steaming and mix gently.
  3. Pour into a greased tray and steam for 15-20 minutes.
  4. Prepare tempering with oil, mustard seeds, curry leaves, and chilies. Mix sugar-water and pour over dhokla.
  5. Garnish with coriander and serve with green chutney.
