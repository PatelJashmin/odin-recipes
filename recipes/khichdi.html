Detailed Khichdi Recipe

About this Recipe

A comforting one-pot dish that blends rice, moong dal, and mixed veggies with spices like turmeric and garam masala—easy to make and full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • Rice, moong dal, oil (or coconut oil), cumin seeds
  • Onion, curry leaves, ginger, tomato puree
  • Mixed vegetables
  • Turmeric, cayenne or paprika, coriander powder, garam masala, salt
  • Cilantro for garnish

Steps

  1. Sauté onion, spices, and tomato puree.
  2. Add vegetables, rice, dal; mix and add water.
  3. Cook until soft (Instant Pot, pressure cooker, or stovetop).
  4. Stir in peas and garam masala; garnish with cilantro. Serve hot.
